ABOUT US

DreamWorks Systems Solution Limited (DSSL) is a private limited liability company, duly registered by Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) to carry on the business of providing Information, systems and solution and incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990.
DreamWorks is owned 100% by Nigerian investors, professionals, experts and individuals among whom are academicians and entrepreneurs. It also has foreign partners who are our consultants
We have a customer database of 200, which includes individuals, corporate organization, ministries, departments, agencies, extraministerial departments, businesses and security agencies.
DreamWorks is made up of four units, which includes DreamWorks Consulting Services, DreamWorks Publishing Services, DreamWorks Systems & Solution and DreamWorks Initiative.
